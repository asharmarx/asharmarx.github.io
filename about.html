hello

Welcome to my website. I decided to make this website while I was searching for my very first adult job. I figured, why not teach myself something new while having to rewrite everything from my resume in to a job application after uploading it. At first, I thought it would be easy, I mean it's only HTML but, then I learned about CSS, and then I learned about JavaScript, and then I learned about libraries such as Bootstrap and jQuery, and the list goes on. But, I was glad I started the project as it was a very nice distraction from feeling poor.
